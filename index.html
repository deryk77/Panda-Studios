About US

Your audience forms an opinion about your organisation in under three seconds. Before a proposal is read. Before a meeting is scheduled. Before a single word is spoken — perception has already been set. That perception comes entirely from your brand.

Panda Studios is a brand identity and creative agency based in Kampala, Uganda, working with corporates, NGOs, co-founders and institutions across East Africa. We don't decorate — we build communication systems. Visual identities, websites and campaigns designed with one purpose: to position your organisation as the clear, trusted choice in the minds of the people whose decisions matter most to you.

Design is not decoration. It is the language your organisation speaks before you do.
East Africa's leading brand identity & design agency —
built for leaders who demand more.
