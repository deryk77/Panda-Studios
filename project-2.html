Conservation and Awareness!

Uganda Wildlife Authority (UWA) protects some of Africa's most remarkable ecosystems — but conservation only succeeds when people care. Our brief was to translate UWA's mission into a campaign language that could move a general public audience: stopping thumbs, shifting perceptions, and making wildlife conservation feel personal and urgent rather than institutional.

We designed a series of social media campaigns, digital banners and print materials that spoke in UWA's voice — authoritative but accessible, urgent but not alarmist. Each asset was built to perform across platforms and contexts, from Instagram awareness posts to roadside campaign formats. The through-line: Uganda's wildlife is worth protecting, and everyone has a role to play.
